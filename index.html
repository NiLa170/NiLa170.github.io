Examining the Association Between Vehicle Break-Ins (‘Bipping’) and Theft Trends in San Francisco

San Francisco's crime data presents the development of different crime types over time in detail. We filtered the data to focus on a group of categories named *Personal Focus Crimes*, which include various types of crimes such as assault, robbery, and larceny/ theft. Among these, larceny/ theft stands out as the most frequent crime, and showing the most interesting development over the last two decades.
